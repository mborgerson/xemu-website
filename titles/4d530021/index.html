compat_comments: I can confirm that Crimson Skies: High Road to Revenge is playable from start to finish with no apparent game-breaking bugs or crashes.
The following deviations and bugs are listed:

-3D audio does not play correctly, and volume fadeout does not appear to conform correctly. Additionally, doppler pitch shifting appears to be broken.
-Some specific 2D and 3D audio sound muffled, compared to the original game. The most obvious are weapon firing sounds, specifically Gun_2D.wav
-There has been only one issue found with shader setups. The most obvious is the afterburner 'mirror image' effect which creates a 'ghost' plane on the right of the player's own.
-Varying performance in specific levels. Chicago has the worst performance hit, followed by the Lost City, Arixo, Najavo Nation, Sea Haven and the opening tutorial with the least performance drops.

Outside of the issues listed, the game is playable from start to finish with minor issues.
